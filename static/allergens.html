Food & allergen guide

What's served each day, with allergen data pulled directly from each product's Solucious label — solid tags are confirmed ingredients, dashed tags are cross-contamination trace warnings only.

⚠️ Please read this before relying on it

Every packaged ingredient below was checked against its actual Solucious product label (2026-07-20) — this isn't a guess. Solid tags mean the allergen is an actual ingredient ("Bevat"). Dashed tags mean the label only warns of possible traces from shared equipment ("Kan sporen bevatten van") — not an ingredient, but worth knowing if your allergy is severe.

What this can't cover: fresh unpackaged produce (onion, garlic, herbs), substitutions the kitchen makes on the day, and cross-contact between dishes cooked in the same pots. If you have a real allergy — not just a preference — this page is a starting point for a conversation, not a substitute for one.

Get in touch before your ticket is confirmed — contact@roguerope.be — so we can plan around gluten-free, allergy, or other specific dietary needs. In some cases you can get kitchen access to prep your own food.

Quick answers: tomato & meat type

The two things people ask about most that aren't official allergens — added by guest request.

🍅 Which dishes have tomato?

Yes: Tue Pasta (passata base), Wed Curry (diced tomatoes in the base — paste itself doesn't have it), Thu hummus plate (cherry tomatoes, skippable) & Wraps (fajita sauce + fresh topping), Fri both soups & salsa, all-week fruit bowl (cherry tomatoes).
No: Wed cheese/pie, Thu trail mix/biscuits, Sat chips/cookies/cake. Pesto itself has none.

🥩 What meat is served when?

Tue Pasta: fish (tuna) only. Wed Curry: chicken (100%), no pork. Thu Wraps: pork mince (80%) — take the vegetarian veg-pan line instead if that's an issue. Fri tomato soup: chicken + turkey meatballs, no pork. Fri BBQ: unconfirmed, ask the crew. Sat Pizza: tailorable, just ask for no pork toppings.

Plate, bowl, cutlery, reusable cup (no glass) — we don't do disposables.

## The Winter Edition Vision

As we move indoors for our winter gathering, we're creating something intentionally different:

### Intimacy by Design
Smaller spaces. Shared accommodations. Longer conversations. The cold outside draws us closer together inside, fostering bonds that outdoor summer camps simply can't match.

### Ritual Over Routine
Every workshop, every practice session, every meal becomes an opportunity for presence. We slow down. We notice. We honor the sacredness in the mundane.

### Warmth in Darkness
Winter asks us to turn inward. To rest. To reflect. Our retreat honors this seasonal wisdom, creating space not just for doing, but for being.

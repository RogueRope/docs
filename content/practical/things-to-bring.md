## Required

- Ticket (with QR code)
- ID/Passport
- Reusable cup (no glass)

---

## Comfort (Bunk)

- Bedding (sheets, sleeping bag, blankets)
- Pillow
- Earplugs
- Sleep mask
- Phone charger
- Power strip

---

## Personal Care

- Biodegradable soap + shampoo
- Toothbrush + toothpaste
- Towel
- Deodorant
- Medications (prescription + OTC painkillers/allergy meds)
- First aid basics (band-aids, antiseptic)

---

## Clothes

- Comfortable layers
- Underwear + socks
- Slippers (shared floors)
- Play party outfit (anything goes)
- Warm layer

---

## Food & Drink

- Water bottle
- Favorite snacks/drinks (we provide coffee, water, meals)
- Cooler bag (optional; we provide ice)

---

## Rope & Play

**Rope:**
- 4x8m natural fiber (jute/hemp/cotton) OR synthetic
- Rope scissors or safety shears
- Rope bag

**Play items (optional):**
- Floggers, blindfolds, toys
- Massage oils/lubes (water-based)
- Aftercare items (blankets, snacks, water)

---

## Tech & Reflection

- Camera (if you want photos)
- Journal + pen
- Books/cards for downtime

---

## Don't Bring

- Glass
- Illegal substances
- Weapons
- Pets
- Excessive alcohol
- Camera drones

---

## If You Forget It

Borrow it. We have extras (earplugs, sleep masks, basic toiletries, towels). Don't stress.

---

## Packing Tip

Pack a week before. Use this checklist. Leave room for things you'll return with.

Rogue Rope Camp is a rope gathering, but it’s not a rope show.

We value emotional depth over technical mastery. Skill is welcome; virtuosity is not the point. We’re here for presence, consent, and the kind of connection you can’t fake.

{{< callout type="info" >}}
This camp works when everyone participates. That includes helping build culture: asking, checking in, naming limits, and making room for “no” without punishment.
{{< /callout >}}

While our summer events had the charm of camping under the stars, "Bottoms Up!" offers the comfort of bunk beds within the venue. We understand the importance of personal space and preferences, so we're providing options for both co-ed and separated rooms.

### Room Allocation:

- We'll be designating rooms based on preferences, ensuring everyone feels comfortable and secure.
- Each bunk bed will come with basic bedding. You're welcome to bring any additional personal comfort items.

### Facilities:

- Indoor toilet and shower facilities are available. They're basic but clean and functional.
- Power outlets are available in communal areas, so you can charge your devices when needed. Remember, this is a time to connect with others, so maybe give that phone a bit of a break!

### What to Bring:

- Personal toiletries and any other hygiene products you'll need.
- A small night light or reading light if you prefer.
- Snacks or any personal food items. While we'll be providing meals, it's always good to have your favorites on hand.

**Sleeping Arrangements**:

While the focus is on bunk bed accommodations, if you have any special requirements or concerns, please [reach out to us in advance]({{< ref "/docs/main/contact" >}} "Contact us"). Our aim is to ensure every participant feels comfortable and can focus on the experience.

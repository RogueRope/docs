For those who attended our summer event, here are the notable changes for "Bottoms Up!":

## Infra

* Transition to an Indoor Setting! 
* Expanded 400 m² rope space.
* A different vibe: Cozy bunk beds replacing tents!

Building on the feedback and experiences from our summer event, we've made purposeful tweaks to curate a distinct yet familiar feel for "Bottoms Up!". While certain elements have shifted to fit the indoor context, our core ethos remains unchanged.

Our commitment to emphasizing the journey of the bottom in rope continues to be at the forefront. By maintaining a focus on sensory and emotional explorations, we aim to make this indoor gathering as enriching and transformative as our outdoor adventures.

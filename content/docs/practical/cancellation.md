## If We Have to Cancel

If Rogue Rope Camp can't legally take place — due to force majeure, venue issues, or anything else outside our control — we'll cancel the event. If that happens, **you'll get a full refund.** No hidden fees. No penalties. If you paid X euros, we return X euros.

---

## If You Need to Cancel

Things come up. We get it.

### Reselling or Gifting Your Ticket

You're welcome to pass your ticket on — but please check with us first.  
Every participant needs to be confirmed by the organizers, so don't sell your ticket to someone we wouldn't be able to welcome. Send us an email with your replacement's **FetLife name** and **email**, and we'll follow up with both of you.

### Cancelling Directly

- **More than 3 weeks before camp (before July 14, 2026):** Cancel for free — contact us and we'll arrange the refund.
- **Less than 3 weeks before camp:** If we can fill your spot from the waiting list, we'll refund **90% of your ticket**. If there's no one waiting, we can't issue a refund.

---

## If the Event Is Cut Short

If camp starts but has to end early due to weather, safety issues, or anything else outside our control, we unfortunately can't offer refunds for the remaining days.

We hope it doesn't happen. If it does, we'll handle it with as much care as possible.

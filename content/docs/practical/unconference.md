## A Space for Sharing, Not Showing Off

Our unconference is a lightly held container for spontaneous brilliance, shared wisdom, and weird little things you didn't know you needed to talk about.

There's no set schedule. No official teachers. No hierarchy.  
Just a board, some post-its, and a collective invitation to explore together.

---

## The Four Unconference Rules

1. Whoever shows up are the right people.
2. Whatever happens is the only thing that could have.
3. Whenever it starts is the right time.
4. It's over when it's over.

That's it. That's the magic.

---

## What Might You Offer?

You don't need to be an expert. You just need to care.

What could you bring?
- A conversation you're hungry to have?
- A practice or technique you'd love to share?
- A rope-related body puzzle?
- A hot take on power dynamics, neurodivergence, play, pain, ritual, or consent?
- Something half-formed and strange that's been living in you?

We're here for the offbeat, the personal, the not-quite-finished.  
If you care about it, someone else probably does too.

---

## What Might You Find?

- Group discussions on body reading and negotiation
- Skillshares on suspension, flow states, or aftercare
- Explorations of fear, shame, intimacy, or archetypes
- Conversations that crack you open, in the best way

---

## How It Works

A board goes up Thursday morning. Sessions are proposed with a post-it — topic, time, location.  
Sign up for the ones that call to you.  
Run the one that needs to happen.

Our content crew will support setup and logistics.  
The rest is yours.

Keep an eye out. Listen to what's bubbling in you.  
And when it's ready — share it.

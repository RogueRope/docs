## Build Day Is How We Begin

Rogue Rope Camp doesn't just *appear*.  
It's built — by hands, by hearts, by you.

**Build Day is Tuesday, August 4th.** Arrive anytime from 10:00.

This is your invitation to be part of the beginning.

---

## Why Come Early?

You don't need to be strong. Or skilled. Or organized.  
You just need to show up.

We'll hang lights between trees, set up suspension points, arrange the chill spaces, decorate the land, maybe rig a hidden nook or two. Some people hammer. Some sweep. Some make tea and tell stories. All of it counts.

By the time camp officially opens on Wednesday, the people who built it are already woven together. You'll know each other's names. You'll have laughed. You'll feel at home before the gates open.

---

## How It Works

- **Arrive anytime from 10:00 on Tuesday, August 4th**
- No sign-up needed. No special skills. Just show up and ask what's needed — someone will point you toward tools, tasks, or tunes.
- We'll use a shared WhatsApp group to coordinate loosely
- **Work + Play:** Tuesday is for building. Play begins Wednesday evening after dinner.

---

## Practical Notes

- Bring clothes you don't mind getting dusty or sweaty
- Dinner is provided on Tuesday evening for early arrivals
- Camping is available from Tuesday — pitch your tent and settle in
- You're welcome to arrive at any point on Tuesday, even if you can only contribute a few hours

---

This isn't just setup.  
It's a ritual. A soft beginning.  
The first knot in something unforgettable.

Come build it with us.

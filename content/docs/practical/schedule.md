## Overview

Rogue Rope Camp does not run on a packed timetable.  
But the week does have a clear structure.

- **Tuesday:** Build Day and early arrival
- **Wednesday:** main arrival day
- **Wednesday 21:00:** Opening Circle
- **Thursday morning:** mandatory Consent Moment
- **Thursday to Saturday:** camp in full motion
- **Sunday:** closing, packing, and departures

## Tuesday — Build Day

Tuesday is for setup and early arrival.

If you want to help build the camp, decorate the space, move things around, or simply arrive more slowly, this is the day to come.

Build Day is optional, but it is a good way to land gently and meet people before the full camp begins.

---

## Wednesday — Arrival and Opening

Wednesday is the main arrival day.

Please make sure you are **on site by Wednesday evening** so we can begin together.

Use the day to arrive, set up your tent, settle in, and get your bearings.

### Fixed moment
**21:00 — Opening Circle**

This is the official start of camp.  
Please be there, and please be on time.

After the Opening Circle, the space opens and the first evening begins.

---

## Thursday — Consent and First Full Day

Thursday is the first full day of camp.

### Fixed moment
**Morning — Consent Moment**

This is mandatory for everyone.

After that, the camp opens up more fully and finds its rhythm.

You can expect a mix of peer-led offerings, spontaneous sessions, rope and BDSM spaces, quiet time, connection activities, and some larger rituals, plays, or happenings held by the organizing team.

---

## Friday and Saturday — Open Camp Days

These are the main camp days.

There is no rigid program from morning to night, but the space will be active and alive.

Some things will be announced. Some things will happen organically. Some things will be held by participants. Some things will be held by the organizing team.

Expect space for rope, BDSM, erotic exploration, rest, conversation, wandering, watching, joining in, or stepping back.

---

## Sunday — Closing and Departure

Sunday is for winding down, packing up, and saying goodbye.

Some people leave early in the day. Some people may need to leave earlier in the weekend. That is okay.

We ask people to begin together.  
We trust people to leave when they need to.

---

## What is fixed

These are the key moments to remember:

- **Tuesday:** optional Build Day and early arrival
- **Wednesday evening:** everyone should be on site
- **Wednesday 21:00:** Opening Circle
- **Thursday morning:** mandatory Consent Moment
- **Thursday to Saturday:** open camp days with peer-led and organizing team-held moments
- **Sunday:** closing and departures

Everything else stays relatively open by design.

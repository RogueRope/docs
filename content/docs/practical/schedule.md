## 📅 Tuesday–Sunday, 4–9 August 2026

**6 Days of Rope, Ritual & Connection**

---

## The Rhythm of Our Time

This isn't a traditional event with back-to-back workshops.  
Our six days have a **gentle, held structure** that makes room for spontaneity, presence, and deep connection.

Each day has:
- A **morning rhythm** — slow coffee, optional movement, people finding each other
- **Open space** — for tying, resting, exploring, connecting
- **Unconference sessions** — peer-led, emergent, yours to shape
- **Shared meals** and informal time
- **Evening offerings** — ceremony, play party, ritual, or rest, depending on the night

---

## The Arc

### **Tuesday — Build Day**

_Arrival from 10:00. No program. Just presence._

Come early if you can. Help hang lights. Set up suspension points. Arrange the space. Make coffee. Make friends.  
This is one of the best ways to arrive — you'll already feel at home by the time camp officially opens.

Play begins **after dinner on Wednesday**. Tuesday is for building.

---

### **Wednesday — Arrival & Opening**

_Main arrival from 14:00. Opening Circle at 21:00 — mandatory._

Check in, find your spot, pitch your tent. Dinner together as we arrive.  
The **Opening Circle** at 21:00 sets the container for everything that follows. Be there.

---

### **Thursday — First Full Day**

_The space comes alive._

Wake slowly. Eat together. The unconference board goes up — add your session, claim your time slot.  
Tying, connecting, wandering, resting. Let the day take you.  
Evening: open play space.

---

### **Friday — Deep Dive**

_Often the most intense day._

The camp has found its rhythm. Connections have deepened. Courage is higher.  
Afternoon: open tying space and unconference sessions.  
Evening: the **Main Ceremony** — our central shared ritual of the camp.

---

### **Saturday — The Peak**

_The most alive day._

Morning movement, lazy breakfast, mid-day sunshine.  
Afternoon: final unconference slots, last sessions.  
Evening: **Play Party** — our celebration of everything we've built together. Dress code applies.

---

### **Sunday — Emergence & Closing**

_The hardest and most important day._

We slow down together. The **Closing Circle** gathers everyone for gratitude, integration, and goodbye.  
Teardown begins after the circle. Checkout by 15:00.  
[Leave No Trace](leave-no-trace) — the land should rest better for having held us.

## A Note on the Unconference

Sessions are proposed and organized by you, the participants.  
A board goes up Thursday morning. Put your session on it. Sign up for others.  
No hierarchy. No gatekeeping. Just collective wisdom shared between people who care.
